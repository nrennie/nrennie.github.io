Research

I am interested in the application of mathematical and statistical techniques to real-world problems, in order to improve decision making. My primary research interest lies in the domain of revenue management. Revenue management is common in industries such as transport and hospitality, and, as such, I have worked in collaboration with Deutsche Bahn, Lufthansa Group, and Swiss Air Lines throughout my research.

Currently, my research focuses on developing statistical methods which highlight deviations between real-world observations and the expected behaviour of revenue management systems.
